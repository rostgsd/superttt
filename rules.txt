How to play Super Tic Tac Toe

Basics
The board contains nine games of tic tac toe. A player wins by winning 3
games of tic tac toe in a row, column or diagonal.

Playing a turn
The first player picks a game to play in and makes a move in that game, the game the next player
gets to play in corresponds to the move location of the last player, i.e. if X choses to play
in the middle square in the upper left game, then O must play in the middle game. If a game
the opponent is supposed to play is already won or a draw, then the opppoent can chose the
game for their move.

Opponent options
You can play locally with another person or wtih programmed AI, toggle in settings.
